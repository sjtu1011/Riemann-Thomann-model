The Riemann-Thomann Model

Understanding Leadership Through Four Basic Orientations

通过四种基本取向理解领导力

Origin / 起源

Developed by Christoph Thomann
based on Fritz Riemann’s personality theory

由克里斯托夫·托曼提出
基于弗里茨·黎曼的人格理论

Four Basic Orientations / 四大基本取向


变革 ⚡ Stability
稳定 ⚓ Distance
距离 🏝️ Proximity
亲近 ❤️

Innovator
